## Business Volume or BV and PV

BV stands for Business Volume, This is the point system of the Charge App that uses to give rewards to user in the higher ranks.

BV is also used in Binary Tree to calculate the total BV of an Organization or Network.

PV stands for Personal Volume, The only to aquire PV is buying directly to MyStore a product that has a BV points.
